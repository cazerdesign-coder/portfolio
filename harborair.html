Planting the Seeds

Harbor Air is a small regional airline located in New England. The airline was looking to create an online booking site with a modern, trustworthy experience that reflected the charm and reliability of a local business.

The Goal

Create a visually pleasing and intuitive booking platform with a cohesive and easy-to-apply design system

Key Problems

  • Unintuitive with confusing navigation
  • Not user friendly
  • Visually outdated and overly dense with information
  • Inconsistent styles with buttons and layouts
