Tending the Garden

Every great garden needs care, patience, and the right tools.

I'm Sam Cazer, a UI/UX designer studying New Media Design at RIT. I specialize in creating intuitive, scalable digital experiences that merge creativity with technology. My work explores how thoughtful design systems and emerging AI tools can make interfaces more human-centered, efficient, and engaging. Whether I'm prototyping interactive products or refining design systems, I focus on building experiences that feel seamless, accessible, and meaningful.

Besides my work, I create many crafts through crocheting, jewelry making, and illustration. I also love drinking tea!
