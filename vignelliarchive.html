Health Tech Platform

Planting seeds for better patient care

Role Product Designer
Timeline 6 Weeks
Team 2 Designers

Overview

A major healthcare provider needed to modernize their patient portal and provider dashboard. The existing system was fragmented and difficult to navigate. I led the redesign to create a unified, intuitive experience that helped both patients and providers flourish.

The Challenge

The existing platform had a 68% abandonment rate on critical tasks. Patients found it confusing, and providers spent too much time clicking through systems. We needed to clear the weeds and plant something that would actually grow.

Key Problems

  • High abandonment rates on critical user flows
  • Outdated interface causing trust issues
  • Complex navigation preventing task completion
  • No mobile experience in an increasingly mobile world
